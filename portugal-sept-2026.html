Group Notes
Before We Go

🥂 Non-Drinker

One traveler doesn't drink. All selected venues have great non-alcoholic options — Sumol passionfruit soda, fresh-squeezed juices, sparkling water. Portugal makes it easy and nobody makes it weird.

🐟 Non-Seafood Eater

One traveler doesn't eat seafood. Most Portuguese menus include strong meat dishes alongside seafood. See ⚠️ CALL AHEAD flags in the itinerary for spots that need confirming before you arrive.

Aug 31
Arrive Faro Easy arrival day
Morning
Land at 9:10am. Check into Hotel Faro & Beach Club, eat, decompress. You've been traveling since the night before — protect this morning.

Rooftop drinks at the hotel, then dinner at O Alminhas.

07
Sept 6
Full Lisbon Day Belém · Alfama · Fado
Morning — Belém
Take the 15E tram from Praça do Comércio along the river. Stop at Pastéis de Belém first — the original pastel de nata (yes, touristy, yes, worth it). Then Mosteiro dos Jerónimos (~€10, book online ahead), and a walk to the Torre de Belém for photos.
Midday
Tram back, lunch at a local tasca in Alfama — ask your hotel for a current recommendation the morning of.
Afternoon — Alfama
Get lost in Lisbon's oldest neighborhood. Narrow lanes, laundry strung between windows, fado drifting from doorways. Climb to Miradouro de Santa Luzia, then up to Miradouro da Senhora do Monte — the best panorama in the city. Arrive 30–45 min before sunset.
⚠️ Watch your pockets in Alfama and on Tram 28 — both are well-known pickpocket spots targeting tourists.
📍 Fado Show — Book 1–2 Weeks in Advance

Dinner + show: ~€40–80/person · Show only: ~€15–30/person · Full dinner menu at all venues ✓

Entry Requirements
Docs & Border Info

🛂 EES — Active & Affects Your Trip

The EU Entry/Exit System launched April 10, 2026. On arrival at Faro, you'll submit fingerprints and a facial scan at passport control — nothing to do beforehand, it happens automatically. Expect slightly longer queues while the system is still new.

✅ ETIAS — Not Required for September 2026

The EU pre-travel authorization system (similar to the US ESTA, €20, valid 3 years) is expected to launch Q4 2026 with a 6-month transition period. Won't be mandatory until ~April 2027. No action needed for this trip.

Passport
Verify valid through March 8, 2027 (6 months past Sept 8 departure)
Visa
Not required for US passport holders (Schengen, up to 90 days)
Currency
Euro. Cards widely accepted. Bring cash for Casinha do Petisco and Taberna da Rua das Flores (cash only).
Tipping
Not expected. Rounding up or leaving €1–2 is appreciated.
Rideshare
Uber works in Lisbon and major Algarve towns.
Language
English widely spoken, especially in tourist areas and among younger Portuguese.
