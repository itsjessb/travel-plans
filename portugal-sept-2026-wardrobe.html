Rules
Your Body Rules (Non-Negotiable)
Silhouette: Hourglass, 5'3.5", short torso, long legs — always define the waist.
Necklines: Scoop or U-neck default. Never high-neck or crew.
Rise: Mid-rise on everything.
Tops: Cropped or tucked — never untucked and flowy on top with volume on bottom.
Fabrics: Natural only — linen, cotton, Tencel, bamboo.
Jewelry: Gold only.
Skirt lengths: Knee (best) or floor-length maxi. NEVER mid-calf midi.
Rib flare rule: Nothing cuts horizontally across the widest rib point — pieces must skim, not cut.
Shoe principle: Build outfit in neutrals first. The interesting shoe is the punctuation, added last.
Church coverage: Mosteiro dos Jerónimos, Igreja de Santo António (Lagos), Faro Cathedral all require covered shoulders. Your linen scarf handles all three.
